Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-02-08

WOOP Minute is designed to work fully offline. We do not require accounts and we do not run analytics or tracking.

Data collection

We do not collect, transmit, or sell personal data. All WOOP entries you create are stored locally on your device using Apple’s local storage (SwiftData).

Sharing

If you choose to share a WOOP (e.g., via the iOS share sheet), the content is shared only through the method you select.

Contact

If you have privacy questions, contact: woopminute@icloud.com
