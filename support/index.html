FAQ

  • Is WOOP Minute offline? Yes — fully offline. No accounts, no backend.
  • Do you collect any data? No. Your WOOPs stay on your device.
  • How do widgets work? Widgets show your active WOOPs as daily reminders.
  • How many active plans can I have? Up to 3 at a time.
  • Can I export/share a plan? Yes — you can copy or share your WOOP.

Troubleshooting

  • Restart the app
  • Update iOS to the latest version
  • Reboot your device
  • If an issue persists: send your iOS version + device model to support
