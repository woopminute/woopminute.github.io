What is WOOP?

WOOP is a four-step process that helps you act when it gets hard — without relying on willpower.

  • Wish — define a concrete, realistic goal
  • Outcome — imagine the best possible result
  • Obstacle — name what typically gets in your way
  • Plan — create an If-Then plan: “If X happens, then I will do Y.”
